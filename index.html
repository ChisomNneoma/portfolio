migration analysis
(excel project)

I was tasked with analyzing the records of a travel agency on the migration of Nigerian citizens in 2022 using Excel. I cleaned up the data using formulas and functions such as text-to-columns for separating gender from name, IFS to assign generation to year of birth. I went on to create pivot tables and charts to summarize the data and make the dashboard creation easier. On the dashboard, there are charts and graphs showing the generation with the highest migration rate, the states with the highest and lowest migration rate, the top travel destinations based on gender and education level, and the most used airlines.
